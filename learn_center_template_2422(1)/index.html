Welcome to COdel

CODel is an Online Virtual Learning System, which serves as a platform through which students learn from anywhere in the world.

The CODel School in the Federal University of Tehnology, simulates a virtual learning enviroment situated online which is accessed through the Internet. The primary benefit of this system is that it gives our student maximum flexibility durring their program. Course Lectures and materials would all be gotten from this platform.
